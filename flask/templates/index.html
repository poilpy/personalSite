Semantic Image Segmentation and Classifier

Semantic image segmentation is the process of using machine learning to identify and label the parts of an image that a user is looking for. Below is a demo of a model which takes in jpg images and creates a segmentation for it just like these examples.

Another model will also classify your image, telling you in words what it thinks your image is about.

Segmentation supports: aeroplane, bicycle, bird, boat, bottle, bus, car, cat, chair, cow, diningtable, dog, horse, motorbike, person, potted plant, sheep, sofa, train, tv/monitor

Classification supports: plane, car, bird, cat, deer, dog, frog, horse, ship, truck
